# Standard watch

## Normal

Since Errich is the only character who cannot see in the dark, 
the watch has him take the first and last watch so that he will be able to see
the best.

Errich (1 hr)
Vargan (2 hr)
Krotho (2 hr)
Alidris (2 hr)
Errich (1 hr)
